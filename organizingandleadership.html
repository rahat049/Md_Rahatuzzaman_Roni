Here are some of my leadership and organizing roles in various organizations.

KILO FLIGHT

  • Captain (2022-Present)
  • Team Manager (2021-2022)
  • Electrical Control & Safety System Expert (2020-2021)
“Kilo Flight” is a Formula Student team consisting of dedicated and hardworking young individuals. Formula Student is one of the most renowned educational engineering competitions, backed by several motorsports industries like TESLA, BMW, etc. The vision of the team is to innovate young engineers and encourage more young people to pursue their careers in engineering. Team “Kilo Flight” is the first formula student team from Khulna University of Engineering & Technology, Bangladesh to participate in the most prestigious competitions, FORMULA STUDENT UK 2020, 2021, 2022 and FORMULA STUDENT GERMANY 2022 securing a satisfactory position which has also brought huge national media attention to the university and encouraged more students to pursue their dream of engineering.

LOOP

  • Chief Executive Officer (2023-Present)
  • Assistant Content & Training Officer (2022-2023)
LOOP (Control Engineering Club) is an organization of Khulna University of Engineering & Technology. It mainly deals with Programming, Robotics & Industrial Automation. It holds several workshops and seminars every year on Industrial Automation (PLC, HMI, VFD), basic Programming and Introduction to robotics.

IMechE KUET Student Chapter

  • Assistant General Secretary (2021-2023)
IMechE KUET Student Chapter is the first-ever student chapter of IMechE in Bangladesh. It is working under the supervision of IMechE Bangladesh. About IMechE or Institution of Mechanical Engineers, throughout the constantly evolving work of mechanical engineers, IMechE has been at the heart of the world's most important and dynamic industries since its formation in 1847. The Institution is the largest network of mechanical engineers for knowledge, skill, and opportunity throughout the world.

CADers

  • Assistant Event Manager (2020-2022)
‘CADers’ is an active CAD based organization of Khulna University of Engineering & Technology, Khulna which has been working with Computer Aided Engineers since 2010. This is the first ever organization in Bangladesh to start working at CAD sectors of Bangladesh. To spread the importance of Computer Aided Engineering to the utmost ‘CADers’ has been working to nurture the young Design Engineers. Engineering students of 2nd & 3rd year of Khulna University of Engineering & Technology generally form the organization body.
